Account Deletion — QuotelyAI

Last updated: November 6, 2025

Summary

You can delete your QuotelyAI account anytime. Deleting your account will permanently remove your authentication profile and user-associated data from our Firebase services. This page explains the in-app deletion steps and provides a web request option to meet Google Play’s data deletion policy.

TL;DR: Delete inside the app at Profile → Delete Account, or email us at akdroidlabs@gmail.com. We complete requests within 7 business days.

Delete your account inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the QuotelyAI app and sign in (if needed).
  2. Go to Profile → Delete Account.
  3. Read the confirmation, then tap Delete.

We immediately submit a deletion request to Firebase. Once processed, your account and linked data are permanently removed and can’t be recovered.

Request deletion via email (web option)

If you no longer have the app or cannot access your account, email us:

Send deletion request

Include your registered email to help us verify ownership. We may reply to confirm before completing deletion.

Processing time: we aim to complete email requests within 7 business days.

What gets deleted

Removed permanently

  • Firebase Authentication user (email/Google sign-in identity)
  • User data tied to your UID (e.g., favorites, saved items)
  • User-owned files in Firebase (if any were created by you)

May be retained (non-identifying)

  • Aggregated or anonymous analytics (not linked to your identity)
  • Operational logs & backups for security and reliability

Backups: some systems keep time-limited encrypted backups. When backups expire or are rotated, your deleted data is purged. We do not restore deleted accounts.

If you re-install or sign up again

After deletion, your previous account and data cannot be recovered. You can create a new account anytime using the app’s sign-in screen.

Verification & safety

  • We may ask you to confirm your registered email to prevent unauthorized requests.
  • We act on in-app deletions immediately; email requests are processed within 7 business days.
  • We follow Google Play’s data deletion requirements and Firebase best practices.
