Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the QuotelyAI app for mobile devices that was created by AKDroid Labs as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Third Party Access

The Service Provider may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

AI Quote Generation (Gemini API)

The Application uses Google's Gemini API to generate AI-based motivational and creative quotes. When you use features like "Generate Quote", your entered text (prompt) is securely transmitted to Google servers for processing by the Gemini API, and a generated response is returned to your device.

The Service Provider does not store or log any user-entered prompts or generated responses on its own servers. All data exchanged with Gemini API is handled according to Google AI Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

The Gemini API may temporarily log data to improve the quality and reliability of AI models, as stated in Google’s official documentation. However, AKDroid Labs and the QuotelyAI Application do not have access to your Gemini API interaction history.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the app. You may use standard uninstall processes available on your device.
