Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AKDroid Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the QuotelyAI mobile application (“the App”). By using this App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Overview of App Features

QuotelyAI allows users to view, save, customize, and share motivational and creative quotes. It includes:

  • Login using Email or Google (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile section showing your name and Google profile picture
  • Predefined quotes organized by category (stored locally)
  • Ability to generate AI quotes via Google’s Gemini API (Vertex AI)
  • Customization of quote cards with personal images and text
  • Daily quote notifications using Notification Manager

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process only limited information necessary to operate our services:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google or Email, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Data: Includes general app usage, such as app version, session duration, and interactions (like, share, save actions).
  • Device Information: Such as device type, Android version, and general diagnostic logs for crash analytics.

We do not collect or store passwords, contact lists, or precise location data.

3. AI Quote Generation (Gemini API via Vertex AI)

The App uses Google Cloud’s Gemini API to generate AI-based quotes. When you use the “Generate Quote” feature:

  • Your entered prompt text is securely transmitted to our Cloud Run backend (hosted on Google Cloud).
  • Our backend authenticates with Gemini API via a Google-managed Service Account.
  • We do not store your prompt or the generated quote on any of our servers.

All AI requests and responses are handled in compliance with Google AI Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

4. Local Storage and Permissions

The App may request the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • Storage (Read/Write): Required to select images from your gallery or to save customized quote cards to your device.
  • Notification Permission: Used to deliver daily motivational quote notifications to your device.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch AI-generated quotes and online images from Pixabay.

All images used in predefined quote cards are royalty-free and sourced from Pixabay.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with trusted third-party services to provide a seamless experience:

  • Firebase Authentication – User login and identity management
  • Firebase Realtime Database – Storing minimal user preferences or app data
  • Google Vertex AI (Gemini) – AI quote generation
  • Pixabay – For fetching background images

6. Data Retention and User Control

  • Your basic profile data (name, email, photo) remains stored within Firebase as long as your account exists.
  • You can delete your account or data by contacting quotelyai.team@gmail.com.
  • AI prompts and generated responses are not stored by us; they are processed transiently through Google’s servers.

7. Security Measures

We follow industry-standard security practices:

  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Firebase and Google Cloud securely manage all authentication and access control.
  • No sensitive data is shared with unauthorized third parties.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. You are advised to check this page regularly for any updates. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
